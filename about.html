Welcome to N.D.H Cinemas, Gaborone’s favourite movie destination!

We opened in 2024 with one goal: to bring the best movie experience to Botswana. With comfortable seats, the latest sound systems, and the biggest blockbusters, every visit feels like a VIP night at the movies.

Whether you love action, romance, family films, or Nollywood hits — we have something for everyone.
